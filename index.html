Anders Square, Perton

Your local, on the square

Café 29 is a family-run coffee shop in the heart of Perton — right on Anders Square, next to the chip shop.

We serve breakfast, brunch, proper coffee and a counter full of homemade cakes and tray bakes, seven days a week. Our coffee is roasted by Origin, and there's indoor and outdoor seating with room for the whole family.

Whether you're nipping in for your morning flat white, settling in for a long weekend brunch, or grabbing four cakes to take home, there's a seat for you.

On the menu

From the first coffee to the last slice of cake

A few of the things people come back for. Full breakfast, brunch, sandwiches, jacket potatoes and the daily cake counter are all served in-café.

Breakfasts

Full English with Hipkiss butchers sausage, plus small, vegetarian and vegan versions.

Brunch

Brioche French toast, hash benedicts, halloumi stacks and strawberry pancakes.

Smashed avocado

On toasted sourdough with chilli, seeds and chive oil — add smoked salmon.

Sandwiches & toasties

The Club 29, the Italian Job, sourdough toasties — served with salad, slaw and crisps.

Salads & jackets

Fresh salads and loaded jacket potatoes for a lighter lunch.

The cake counter

Freshly baked brownies, blondies, cookies and tray bakes — gluten-free and vegan too.

Menus change with the seasons and there's a specials board most days. Pop in or check Facebook for what's on.
